Startup Mindset

Build fast, learn faster. Obsess over the problem, not the solution. Talk to users weekly. Ship increments, not dreams.

  • Measure outcomes, not output.
  • Design onboarding for one clear action.

Acquisition Funnels

  • Pick one channel to master before diversifying.
  • Track activation → retention → referral.
  • Landing pages win with clarity, not clutter.

Revenue Engines

  • Subscriptions align incentives.
  • Pricing signals value—test unapologetically.
  • Gross margin is your oxygen.

KPIs & Runway

  • Default alive: forecast cash monthly.
  • Board updates: crisp, candid, consistent.
